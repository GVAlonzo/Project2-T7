Top 100 Independent Restaurants
Visualized

We explore the top 100 U.S. Independent Restaurants, a ranking measuring the highest-grossing independent restaurants in 2020. Only restaurant concepts with no more than five locations are considered "independents". Here we take a visual exploration of where they are located and how they compare.
